# Invoicing And Compliance Notes

This is a business planning note, not legal or tax advice. Confirm details with a qualified China and Singapore tax advisor before contracting.

## Core Rule

A Singapore invoice is not a China VAT invoice.

It may support China-side accounting and enterprise income tax documentation when paired with contracts, payment proof, service delivery evidence, and any required tax payment records, but it does not replace a China VAT special invoice.

## Customer Segments By Invoice Requirement

| Customer Type | Contracting Route | Invoice Route |
|---|---|---|
| Cross-border, overseas-facing, or foreign-invested customer | Singapore entity direct contract | Singapore invoice |
| China customer with cross-border procurement process | Singapore entity direct contract | Singapore invoice + documentation pack |
| China customer requiring China VAT invoice | Domestic partner or future China entity | China VAT invoice from domestic party |

## Singapore Direct Documentation Pack

Provide:

- Service contract or order form.
- Statement of Work.
- Singapore invoice.
- Singapore company registration information.
- Monthly usage report.
- Delivery acceptance record.
- Payment receipt or bank remittance confirmation.
- Chinese translation of invoice if needed.
- Tax documentation requested by customer finance team.

## Contract Language

Use service descriptions like:

```text
AI API Gateway SaaS Subscription
AI Gateway Technical Support and Usage Analytics
Model Routing, Billing Management, API Monitoring and Access Control Services
```

Avoid:

```text
OpenAI recharge
Claude recharge
GPT quota top-up
API key resale
Credits resale
One-dollar quota
```

## China Invoice Option

For domestic customers that require China VAT invoices, use:

```text
China customer -> domestic partner signs and invoices
domestic partner -> purchases/uses Singapore gateway service
Singapore company -> invoices domestic partner
```

Suitable domestic partners:

- AI consulting companies.
- Software outsourcing firms.
- Cloud service resellers.
- Enterprise digital transformation service providers.
- Companies with software/service invoicing capability.

Expect to share margin with the domestic partner.

## Provider Compliance Boundary

Invoice compliance does not solve model-provider compliance.

The service must still avoid:

- Selling third-party API keys.
- Transferring third-party credits.
- Sharing accounts or credentials.
- Promising region, payment, or KYC bypass.
- Serving unsupported end-user regions when provider terms prohibit it.

Use this customer-facing language:

```text
We provide AI API gateway SaaS and technical services. We do not sell or transfer third-party API keys, service credits, balances, or accounts. GPT-5.5 usage is enabled only where the customer use case, customer location, end-user location, and model-provider terms allow it.
```

## Suggested Sales Explanation

```text
Our Singapore entity can provide a Singapore invoice for AI API Gateway SaaS and technical services. This is not a China VAT invoice. Customers with cross-border procurement workflows can use it together with the contract, payment proof, usage report, and relevant tax documents for accounting purposes.

If your finance process requires a China VAT invoice, we can route the transaction through a domestic partner or future China entity, and the quote will include local tax and service costs.
```
