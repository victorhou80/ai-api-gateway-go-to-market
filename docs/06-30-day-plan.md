# 30-Day Execution Plan

The first 30 days are for validating customer pain and selling pilots. Do not spend the month expanding model support unless customer conversations prove it is required.

## North Star

Get 1-2 paid pilot deployments and one usable case study.

## Targets

| Metric | 30-Day Target |
|---|---:|
| Qualified leads collected | 300 |
| Direct outreaches sent | 180 |
| Effective replies | 25 |
| Diagnostic calls | 8 |
| Sanitized sample sets received | 5 |
| Assessment reports delivered | 3 |
| Paid pilots | 1-2 |

## Daily Schedule

```text
09:30-10:30 collect 20 new leads
10:30-11:30 send 15-20 customized outreaches
14:00-15:00 follow up previous leads
16:00-17:30 run diagnostics, prepare reports, or demo
```

## Week 1: Build Sales Assets And Lead List

Deliverables:

- One-page positioning PDF or page.
- Demo screenshots.
- Lead tracker.
- Outreach scripts.
- Cost-routing assessment template.
- 100 AI tool site leads.
- 50 implementation-studio leads.

Actions:

- Define ICP and non-ICP.
- Prepare 3 demos:
  - Content workflow.
  - Knowledge assistant routing.
  - Simulated AI tool cost routing.
- Send first 50 outreaches.

## Week 2: Run Conversations

Goals:

- Reach 150 total outreaches.
- Book 3-5 diagnostic calls.
- Obtain at least 2 sample sets.

Actions:

- Follow up day-0 contacts after 2 days.
- Ask for 10-30 sanitized samples.
- Record objections and revise scripts.
- Publish first technical article.

## Week 3: Produce Assessments

Goals:

- Deliver 2-3 cost-routing assessments.
- Quote first paid pilot.

Actions:

- Classify customer requests by model fit.
- Estimate all-GPT vs Kimi+GPT routing cost.
- Propose 7-day pilot.
- Keep finding leads while running assessments.

## Week 4: Close Pilots

Goals:

- Close 1-2 paid pilots.
- Define success metrics before deployment.
- Ask for anonymous case-study permission.

Pilot success metrics:

- Cost reduction estimate or measured reduction.
- Routing accuracy accepted by customer.
- Usage report generated.
- Budget alert configured.
- No critical quality drop on final outputs.

## Weekly Review

Review:

- Which lead source replies most?
- Which ICP converts to diagnostic calls?
- Which objection appears most often?
- Which use cases make Kimi routing obvious?
- Whether price is too high, too low, or unclear.

Do not judge the business by total website traffic. Judge it by diagnostic conversations and sample sets.

## Kill Or Continue Criteria

After 30 days:

Continue if:

- At least 20 effective replies.
- At least 5 diagnostic calls.
- At least 2 serious pilot opportunities.
- At least one segment strongly understands the value.

Revise ICP or messaging if:

- Replies are below 5%.
- Leads only ask for cheap API credits.
- Customers do not understand the routing value.
- No one can provide real samples.

Do not add more models as a default fix. First confirm whether the problem is model coverage, positioning, ICP, or trust.
